[ LOCATION ]

North Oak at Oakvillage

Surrounded by nature, North Oak is located at the corner of Dundas St. E. & Trafalgar Rd. in Oakville. Apart from being loaded with lifestyle amenities, these condos give residents access to over 300 km of immaculate nature walks. Further, the green area encircling the condos includes Bronte Creek Natural Peak, Sixteen Mile Creek. North Oak is well connected to Highways 407, 403, and QEW while the Oakville GO Station is just a few minutes walk. These condos are centrally located amongst malls, shopping centres, diners, schools and hospitals.
